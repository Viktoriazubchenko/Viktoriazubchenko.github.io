About Me

Hi. My name is Viktoria Zubchenko and I'm an enthusiastic and detail-oriented Junior Frontend Developer seeking an entry-level position to use my skills in coding, troubleshooting complex problems, and assisting in the timely completion of projects. I have a firm knowledge of HTML, CSS, JS and responsive design best practices. I also have a good knowledge of frameworks and tools that are used in today’s technology. I am creating websites using HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JS, SCSS, SASS, Bootstrap, Gulp, jQuery, React, Git, AJAX and I am constantly seeking to improve my skills.

I have the ability to adapt to any type of team environment and I feel absolutely comfortable interacting with other team members and clients.

On a personal level I am friendly, highly-motivated, hard-working and I am in love with frontend as it fully satisfies my need for creativity and passion for tech.

You can find out more about my skills by checking my works or my GitHb account.

Thank You.
